Apollo

An upcoming onsite music lesson service, provided by BYU students, to match the busy lives of working professionals

What is Apollo?

Apollo is a service that brings professional music teachers to your office, to offer employees lessons during short breaks throughout the workday

Why at the office?

Many adults would like to learn to play an instrument, but they find that they don't have the time to go to lessons. Well, we've found the solution! We bring the teachers to you! Once a week, take a break to have your lesson with zero inconvenience

For music teachers

Music teachers often struggle with awkward work schedules, such as after kids are done with school, and gaps between students. Struggle no more! As an Apollo affiliate, we match up teachers with businesses to provide full teaching schedules during the workday, offering plenty of students at the same location and during sane work hours, leaving your evenings free!
